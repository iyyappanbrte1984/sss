How to Read This Console (Teacher Script)

This dashboard turns student measurements into SDG 14 indicators: • SDG 14.1 – Reduce marine pollution: – Plastic debris density from shoreline surveys (chart: Plastic Debris Density). – Turbidity trends from water samples (chart: Water Quality Trend).[web:89][web:90] • SDG 14.2 – Protect and restore ecosystems: – Percentage of samples within safe pH, temperature and turbidity ranges. – Area of lakes / rivers monitored by students. • SDG 14.3 – Ocean acidification and climate: – pH readings from local waters, used to discuss global ocean acidification.[web:79][web:110] When students see the pollution line fall and the SDG scores rise, they know their actions (clean‑ups, data collection, awareness campaigns) are truly helping life below water.
